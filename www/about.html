Our Mission

At Modern Web Boilerplate, our mission is to provide developers with a cutting-edge starting point for web projects. We believe in:

  • Accessibility First: Creating interfaces that everyone can use
  • Performance Matters: Building fast experiences on all devices
  • Progressive Enhancement: Ensuring core functionality works everywhere
  • Developer Experience: Making the development process enjoyable
  • Future-Proofing: Adopting modern standards before they become mainstream
